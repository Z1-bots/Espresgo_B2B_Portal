Our idea

Making coffee easier for modern workplaces and events.

Traditional coffee supply can be messy for businesses. Machines need maintenance, capsules create waste, queues take time, and cold drinks often need refrigeration. ESPRESSGO simplifies the process through a compact espresso gel pouch format.

Each pouch is designed to be portable, easy to distribute, and simple to store. That makes it suitable for offices, gyms, event packs, convenience retail, sampling campaigns, and wholesale buyers.
